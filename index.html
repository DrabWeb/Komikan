Bookmarks

Komikan's simple bookmark system lets you mark pages for later.
Komikan lets you add bookmarks in the reader, and then see them in the edit panel.
Click for a quick preview of the page and click again to jump to it.

Editing

Make a mistake when you import your Manga? Don't worry, Komikan's got you covered.
Double click any manga to edit its information(Cover, title, series, artist, writer, tags and file) with a simple UI.
And if you don't want one anymore, you can remove it with one button.

Search and Sort


Single-click Sorting by Title, Series, or Artist
Search by Tags, Title, or both

Import

Import your CBZ / CBR in a few clicks. Import single Manga or mass import an entire series, its made easy in Komikan.
You can tag your manga, set the cover, set the series, artist, and even writer.
Komikan never deletes or moves your original Manga file, so you can store it however you want.

Viewer

Komikan's super fast reader is nice and simple.
With a QuickTime style UI when you hover the window, you can see the information only when you need it.
Easily bookmark and jump to pages and adjust the viewers colours and brightness.
And for those who want to use maximum screen real estate; there is dual page mode.
